Welcome to Post-It!

Here you can post your own notes and view your active and archived notes. When posting a note (up to 160 characters) you can choose to use a title or not. On an active note click the upper right trash can icon to archive it. On an archived note click the upper right retrieve icon to make it an active note again. Feel free to get started and post your own note below!
